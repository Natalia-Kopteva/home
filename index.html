Natalia Kopteva's homepage

I am a Professor at the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Limerick, Ireland, and also serve as the University of Limerick Research Integrity Officer and on the Ethics Committee of the European Mathematical Society.

I hold an M.Sc. in Applied Mathematics and a Ph.D. in Computational Mathematics from Moscow State University. In the past, I held academic positions at Moscow State University, University College Cork, and University of Strathclyde.

I am editor for SIAM Journal on Numerical Analysis and Advances in Computational Mathematics. Including my past and current editorial duties, I have served as editor for 8 international refereed journals.

Research Interests

Numerical Analysis for partial differential equations: a posteriori error estimation, finite element and finite difference methods, singularly perturbed differential equations, semilinear reaction-diffusion, convection-dominated convection-diffusion problems, layer-adapted anisotropic meshes.

See also my profiles at Google Scholar + ResearchGate + arXiv + ORCID (identifier 0000-0001-7477-6926).
